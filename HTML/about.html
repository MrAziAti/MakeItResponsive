Who are we?

We in Inova want to make goods, products and technology easily accessible to everyone. To reach this goal we are frequently hosting innovation camps to share our experience and the information we have acquired over the years, with both companies and regular people. Our company is new, however the team has a lot of expertise and knowledge when it comes to this field of activity. We want to teach everyone that including and supporting developing countries, and producing substitutes to products that benefit the people living in these countries is the way to go.

There are a millions upon millions of people living in poverty or with low income. This however doesn't mean companies should take these markets for granted but instead see it as an opportunity to create or improve products that matter. Inova wants to connect the world through innovation and invite companies to learn about the positive effects of co-creation and communication between developed and undeveloped countries.

Inova wants everyone to understand the strengths and opportunities connected to frugal innovation. To achieve our goals we need companies to be on the same page as us, and we would like to invite anyone who wants to grow their market and company through frugal innovation to join our camps. We hope this sounds interesting and that you could get in touch with us.
